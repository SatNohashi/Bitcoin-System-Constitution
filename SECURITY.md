# Security

If you discover a vulnerability, inconsistency, or exploitable ambiguity in this constitution or its companion standards, please report it responsibly.

## How to Report

Email: satoshinohashi@proton.me

Include:
- Which document and section is affected
- A clear description of the issue
- Why it matters (what could be exploited or misinterpreted)

## What Counts

- Logical contradictions between articles
- Undefined terms carrying constitutional weight
- Loopholes that allow compliant-sounding non-compliance
- Ambiguities an adversarial actor could weaponize

## Response

Reports will be reviewed and, where valid, addressed through constitutional amendment (during the amendment window) or companion standard updates.

This project has no bug bounty program. Reports are voluntary contributions to the commons.

Submissions that do not relate to the constitution or compliant systems will be ignored without response.
