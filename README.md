# Bitcoin System Constitution

A constitutional standard for Bitcoin-aligned systems.

## What This Is

This constitution defines rules for Bitcoin-aligned systems. It establishes three layers with clear roles.

Layer 1 is Bitcoin. Final settlement. The monetary base.

Layer 2 is the cash rail. Fast payments for daily use. Funds must always be withdrawable to Layer 1.

Layer 3 is for smart contracts and experimentation. Optional risk, clearly labeled.

The constitution addresses problems that existing systems often fail to solve: hidden leverage, trapped funds, custodians who cannot prove reserves, governance that never ends, credit disguised as money.

## Core Requirements

Exit is a right. You can always move your funds to a harder layer. No committee required.

Proof, not promises. Any system claiming to hold funds must prove it. Reserves and liabilities, independently verifiable.

Custody must be real. No single key, no single company, no single jurisdiction can seize or lose reserves.

Credit is not money. If it involves lending or counterparty risk, it must be labeled as credit.

Transfer stays simple. Moving funds must work as a plain transfer at the core. No mandatory contract mediation.

Governance dies on schedule. Constitutional amendments close at year 5. Governance decisions close at year 25. Full ossification at year 30.
